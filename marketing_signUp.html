About Igbankwu.com

Igbankwu.com and Omenana Defenders advocates for the cultural preservation of Igbo marriage customs and values

We created Igbankwu.com as an extension of our adorable Facebook platform - Igobo Marry Igbo, to provide the perfect forum for umu Igbo to find each other, meet, court, engage and unite Igbo families through the sacred institution if Igbankwu (marriage) in accordance with the pure tenets of our culture Omenana Igbo, which guides us all as true Igbo sons and daughter

We truly hope you enjoy using this platform just as much as we have enjoyed creating it.

Our Prayer is for Igbankwu to continue to unite and build ndi Igbo through the institution of Igbo marriage aka Igbankwu.com
